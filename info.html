Hideo Kojima

Hideo Kojima is a Japanese video game designer, director, producer, and writer. He is the director of Kojima Productions, which he founded in 2005, and a former vice president of Konami Digital Entertainment. Kojima is the creator, director, and writer of a number of successful video game series, including Metal Gear, Snatcher, and Policenauts.

  • Birthdate: August 24, 1963
  • Birthplace: Setagaya, Tokyo, Japan
  • Occupation: Video Game Designer, Director, Producer, Writer
